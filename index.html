Frequently
Asked Questions

Questions from BeePay users who trust us every day for fast, secure, and seamless digital payments.

How do I send money with BeePay?

Open the app, choose “Send Money,” enter the recipient’s details, and confirm the amount. Transfers are instant and fully encrypted.

What payment methods are supported?

BeePay supports cards, bank transfers, and mobile wallets. You can also pay with QR codes in supported regions.

Is BeePay secure?

Absolutely. All transactions are protected with end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication, and real-time fraud monitoring.

Can I use BeePay internationally?

Yes, BeePay enables cross-border payments in 100+ countries with low conversion rates and instant delivery.

What if I need help with a transaction?

You can reach our support team 24/7 directly through the BeePay app chat or by visiting our Help Center.

Can I cancel a transaction?

If the recipient hasn’t claimed the funds, you can cancel it from the BeePay app dashboard under “Recent Transactions.”
