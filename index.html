Frequently
Asked Questions

Common questions from BeePay users who trust us for secure, instant digital payments every day.

How do I send money with BeePay?

Open the BeePay app, choose “Send Money,” enter the recipient’s details, and confirm the amount. Transfers are instant, secure, and verified in real time.

What payment methods do you accept?

BeePay supports bank transfers, mobile wallets, debit/credit cards, and QR code payments — all in one seamless app experience.

Is my money safe with BeePay?

Absolutely. Every transaction is protected by advanced encryption and AI-driven fraud detection. Your data and funds are always secure.

Can I use BeePay internationally?

Yes! BeePay allows cross-border payments in 100+ countries, with transparent currency conversion and minimal transaction fees.

How long do transactions take?

Instant for BeePay-to-BeePay transfers. External bank or card transactions typically clear within minutes, depending on your provider.

What if I need help with a payment?

Our 24/7 support team is always ready to assist via in-app chat or email. BeePay ensures your issues are resolved quickly and securely.
